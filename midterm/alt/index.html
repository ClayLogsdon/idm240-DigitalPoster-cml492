Interaction Details

Triggers

  • IMG#1&3 - Hovering over the image causes the dragon to fly vertically across the span of the poster.
  • IMG#1 - Clicking anywhere on the poster causes the logo and dragon animations to pause. They animation resumes from where it was paused when clicked again.
  • IMG#2 - Hovering over the poster causes the logo to slide in, first in English, then in Japanese.

Rules

  • Both the dragon and logo animation resume from where they were paused when the poster is clicked again, regardless of where within the poster the click occurs.
  • The dragon will reappear at its starting point once the end point is reached.
  • Once the logo animation ends, it will remain still for 2 seconds as to fully show both the English and Japanese logos.
  • After 2 seconds of the English and Japanese logos being fully shown, the logo animation will restart.
  • The dragon animation will continue while the logo animation is in its two second fully viewable state, which occurs at the end of the logo animation.

  • The dragon will continue to fly across the poster, reappearing at its starting point once the end point is reached, until the animation is paused on click.
